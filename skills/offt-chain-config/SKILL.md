## Activation
- Gather source/target chain IDs, OFT addresses, and messaging endpoints.
- Verify fee quotes and approve required token allowances before configuration.

## Assets
- LayerZero configuration values (chain IDs, endpoints, trusted remotes).
- On-chain OFT contracts and operational runbooks for cross-chain sends.
